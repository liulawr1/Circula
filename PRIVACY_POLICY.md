# Circula Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 23, 2026

Circula is a school-only student marketplace designed to help members of the Head-Royce community trade, sell, give away, and reuse school-related items. This Privacy Policy explains what information Circula collects, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

If you have questions, safety concerns, or privacy requests, please contact support at **circulasupport@gmail.com**.

## Information We Collect

Circula collects information needed to operate the marketplace and keep the community safe.

### Account Information

When you create an account or sign in, Circula may collect:

- Your name
- Your Head-Royce email address
- Your Supabase authentication user ID
- Authentication session information needed to keep you signed in

You may browse public listing information as a guest without creating an account. Circula requires a verified Head-Royce email address for account-based features such as posting, saving, and messaging.

### Listings and Marketplace Content

When you create or interact with listings, Circula may collect:

- Listing titles
- Listing descriptions
- Item categories, conditions, and trade/sale/free preferences
- Listing photos you upload
- Listing status, such as Available, Pending, or Completed
- Saved listings
- Timestamps related to listing activity

Listing content, listing photos, and the listing owner's display name may be visible to guests and signed-in users. The listing owner's school email address is not provided to guests.

### Messages

When you message another user through Circula, the app may collect:

- Message text
- Sender name and email address
- Conversation information
- Message timestamps

Messages are used to help students communicate about listings and on-campus exchanges.

### Reports and Safety Information

If you report a listing, Circula may collect:

- The listing being reported
- The report reason
- Your email address as the reporting user
- Report status and timestamps; guest reports use an anonymous reporter label

Reports are used for moderation and safety review.

### Blocked Users

If you block another user, Circula stores that user’s email address on your device so their listings and conversations can be hidden from your view. Blocking is used for user safety and comfort.

## How We Use Information

Circula uses collected information to:

- Create and manage user accounts
- Verify that users have Head-Royce email addresses
- Let guests browse public marketplace listings
- Display marketplace listings
- Let users save listings
- Let users message listing owners
- Support reporting and moderation
- Allow account deletion
- Improve safety and reliability
- Maintain app functionality across devices

Circula does not use your information for third-party advertising.

## How Information Is Shared

Circula does not sell personal information.

Circula may share or store information with service providers that are necessary for the app to function. Circula currently uses **Supabase** for authentication, database storage, and backend services.

Listings, listing photos, and display names may be visible to guests and signed-in users. Marketplace messages are visible only to the conversation participants.

Reports and safety-related information may be reviewed by the app administrator or school-related moderators when needed to keep the marketplace safe.

## Third-Party Services

Circula uses Supabase to provide backend services, including account authentication, database storage, and message/listing syncing. Supabase may process information according to its own privacy and security practices.

Circula does not include third-party advertising SDKs or third-party tracking SDKs.

## Data Retention

Circula keeps information for as long as needed to operate the app, maintain accounts, support marketplace activity, and address safety or moderation concerns.

When you delete your account in Circula, the app is designed to remove your account and related app data, including listings, saved listings, reports, conversations, and messages from Circula’s backend systems. Some information may remain in backups or logs for a limited period if required for security, troubleshooting, or legal compliance.

## Account Deletion

You can delete your account inside the app:

1. Open **Profile**.
2. Tap **Delete Account**.
3. Confirm deletion.

You can also contact support at **circulasupport@gmail.com** for account or privacy-related help.

## Children and Students

Circula is intended for use by members of the Head-Royce school community. Users should follow school rules and only use the app for appropriate school-related marketplace activity.

## Safety

Circula includes safety features such as:

- Head-Royce email verification
- Listing reporting
- User blocking
- Automated checks for clearly prohibited or unsafe listing and message text
- Community standards
- Account deletion
- On-campus meetup expectations

Users should meet only on campus and should contact a trusted adult or school administrator if anything feels unsafe.

## Security

Circula uses standard network security practices, including HTTPS connections to backend services. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, but Circula aims to protect user information using reasonable safeguards.

## Your Choices

You can:

- Choose what listings and photos you post
- Delete your own listings
- Save or unsave listings
- Block users
- Report unsafe or inappropriate listings
- Sign out of your account
- Delete your account
- Contact support for help

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If important changes are made, the updated policy will be posted with a new “Last updated” date.
